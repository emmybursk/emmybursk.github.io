STEM Content Creation

“Talent is a pursued interest. Anything that you’re willing to practice, you can do"

- Bob Ross

It's a common misconception that mathematical ability is an innate, genetic trait. In reality, this belief becomes a self-fulfilling prophecy. When individuals internalize the idea that they lack inherent aptitude, they are less likely to invest sustained effort, thereby constraining their own development.

My goal is to break this narrative and challenge the belief that scientific ability is fixed rather than developed. As a student who once held this mindset, I now strive to serve as an example that anyone, regardless of background, can overcome those obstacles and succeed in STEM. This is why I began creating STEM content on TikTok. Here, I share my unconventional path to astrophysics, in hopes of inspiring students who feel they are “not smart enough” for STEM, showing them that persistence, resilience, and a willingness to fail and try again can lead to success in fields they once thought were seemingly unattainable.

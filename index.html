Outreach

“Talent is a pursued interest. Anything that you’re willing to practice, you can do"

- Bob Ross

It's a common misconception that mathematical ability is an innate, genetic trait. In reality, this belief becomes a self-fulfilling prophecy. When individuals internalize the idea that they lack inherent aptitude, they are less likely to invest sustained effort, thereby constraining their own development.

I was no exception to this mindset. Struggling with math throughout high school led me to assume that a career in astrophysics was out of reach. When I entered community college, I avoided math-heavy paths, opting for "easier" majors instead. However, my passion for astronomy outweighed my doubts, leading me to accept the challenge, rebuild my mathematical foundation, and pursue astrophysics.

On the social media platform TikTok, I share my unconventional path to astrophysics, in hopes of inspiring students who feel they are “not smart enough” for STEM, showing them that persistence, resilience, and a willingness to fail and try again can lead to success in fields they once thought were seemingly unattainable.

You can follow my science journey on TikTok.

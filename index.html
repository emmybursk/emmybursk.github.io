About Me

Education:

BS, University of Cincinnati, May 2025

AS, Cincinnati State Technical and Community College, May 2023

Research Interests:

Dark matter; large-scale structure; galaxy evolution and formation; strong gravitational lensing; cosmological simulations; galaxy-halo connection; galaxy clusters

I am a recent graduate with a Bachelor of Science in astrophysics and am currently a prospective PhD student. My research interests broadly span cosmology, with experience in the connection between galaxies and dark matter halos, as well as strong gravitational lensing. I am particularly interested in utilizing computational methods, especially hydrodynamical simulations, to explore these topics. Beyond research, I am deeply passionate about science communication and spend much of my free time at museums or out in nature.

Outreach

While I absolutely love doing research, I love talking about it even more! During my time as an intern and astronomy educator at the Cincinnati Observatory, I was fortunate enough to have had the opportunity to give presentations on a variety of astronomy topics, including my own research projects. I find it to be incredibly rewarding having the chance to engage with the public and spark excitement about the universe among visitors of all ages. In addition to this, I also began creating astronomy related content on TikTok in hopes of inspiring the next generation of scientists.
